List of Toys

Toy Car

Toy car is a miniature representation of an automobile used to play for children.

Puzzle

Puzzle is a tiling puzzle that requires the assembly of often oddly shaped interlocking and tessellating pieces.

Teddy Bear

Teddy bear, usually stuffed with soft material and covered with furlike plush.

Flash Cards

A flash cards is a card bearing information, as words or numbers, on either or both sides, used in classroom drills or in private study.

Lego Blocks

Lego bricks/blocks is a line of plastic construction toys that are manufactured by The Lego Group, a privately held company based in Billund, Denmark.

Ball

A ball is used in ball games, where the play of the game follows the state of the ball as it is hit, kicked or thrown by players.

Doodle Pad

Doodle pad is a magnetic drawing toy, consisting of a drawing board, a magnetic stylus, and a few magnet shapes.

Rattle

Rattle is a type of percussion instrument which produces a sound when shaken.

Trampoline

Trampoline is a device consisting of a piece of taut, strong fabric stretched between a steel frame using many coiled springs.

Slide

Playground slides are found in parks, schools, playgrounds and backyards. The slide may be flat, or half cylindrical or tubular to prevent falls.

Swing

Swing is a hanging seat, often found at playgrounds for children, at a circus for acrobats, or on a porch for relaxing, although they may also be items of indoor furniture, such as Latin American hammock or the Indian oonjal.

Doll

Doll is a small-scale figure of a human being used especially as a child's plaything
