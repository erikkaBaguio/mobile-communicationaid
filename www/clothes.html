List of Clothes

T-Shirt

T-shirt is a style of unisex fabric shirt named after the T shape of its body and sleeves.

Pants

Pants are an item of clothing worn from the waist to the ankles, covering both legs separately.

Sweater

Sweater is a garment intended to cover the torso and arms. A jumper is either a pullover or a cardigan, distinguished in that cardigans open at the front while pullovers do not.

Shorts

Shorts are a garment worn over the pelvic area, circling the waist and splitting to cover the upper part of the legs, sometimes extending down to the knees but not covering the entire length of the leg.

Jacket

Jacket is a mid-stomach–length garment for the upper body. A jacket typically has sleeves, and fastens in the front or slightly on the side.

Skirt

Skirt is a woman's outer garment fastened around the waist and hanging down around the legs.

Panty

Panty/panties is a legless underpants worn by women and girls.

Dress

Dress is a one-piece garment for a woman or girl that covers the body and extends down over the legs
